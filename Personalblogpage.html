Author Info

Name: Nefertiti Jackson
Title: CEO/Founder
Current company or school: "How I Make It" blog
Short Bio: Growing up, I never really believed that I could cook. All I knew how to make growing up was spaghetti, hot dogs, basic tacos and fried chicken. Most of the time I would buy food for myself rather than cook. When I got married, my husband showed appreciation for the basic meals that I made for him and that is what inspired me to learn more about cooking. Then I realized I really liked doing it. Over the next few years, I started learning different recipes to cook for my family and I started getting better and better. I began to believe that I could cook. If I followed a recipe, I could spin it in my own direction and customize it to my own taste. One day in 2021, my husband was making red beans and rice and I realized there was no cornbread to go with it. I decided to make some cornbread, but then I also realized "why not record this process and post it on Tik Tok"? Once I posted the video on Tik Tok, not thinking many people would view it, in total, it got almost 500,000 views! With all of the positive comments that kept coming in, I decided to post other easy recipes I like to make for myself or my family. The best part about the recipes are that ANYONE can do it! I even teach my 10 and 11 year old daughters how to cook! My goal is to inspire people to believe they can cook. All it takes is a recipe and a little faith and anything can be possible.
